# FAQs
- What is the GTA Lorcana League?
 - A seasonal league that will track players' performances and top performers will be invited to a **free** Invitational before the next set release
- Who is organizing this?
 - @gtalorcana along with some other volunteers
- How long is a season?
 - A season lasts 7 weeks starting with a new set's release and ending the day before Set Championships start.
- Where and when will the Invitational take place?
 - Enter The Battlefield has graciously partnered with us to host the Invitational.  The Invitational is set to happen on the weekend before the next set release.
- How do I join?
 - Simply participate in your store's constructed tournaments, then have someone report the event results at #results-reporting 
- What does my store need to do?
 - Stores will need to run tournaments that will have rounds and match results recorded.
 - Prizing and structure of these weeklies are up to the store.
- What stores are participating in this league?
 - Any store can participate as long as someone is able to get the results reported.

## Scoring
**Core Constructed** Tournaments only
3 points per Match Win
1 point per Match Draw
0 points per Match Loss
(i.e. 3W-0L will get 9 points, 2W-1D will get 7 points)

Your final score will be your **ten best performances during the season**.

Stores who are able to run four rounds are welcome to. 4-0's will be awarded 12 points.
Similarly, stores that run 5 round swiss + top cuts, all those match wins will count!

## Tiebreakers for Scoring
1st - (Same point) -> whoever reaches the point first
Alice has 50 points, wins 0 points on 06-01, to a total of 50 points.
Bob has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-02, to a total of 50 points.

Alice has the higher tiebreaker of getting to 50 points first.

2nd - (Same point, same day) -> whoever reported their result first
Alice has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-01 at Store One, to a total of 50 points.
Bob has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-01 at Store Two, to a total of 50 points.
Store One is reported at 11:58pm, Store Two is reported at 11:59pm.

Alice has the higher tiebreaker of her store reporting at 11:58pm.

3rd. - (Same points, same day, same store) -> whoever had the better ranking the day.
Alice has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-01 at Store One with an OMW% of 70%, to a total of 50 points.
Bob has 44 points, wins 6 points on 06-01 at Store One with an OMW% of 69%, to a total of 50 points.

Alice has the higher tiebreaker of her getting 9 points on the day.

3rd. - (Same points, same day, same store, same points earned) -> whoever had the better ranking the day.
Alice has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-01 at Store One with an OMW% of 70%, to a total of 50 points.
Bob has 41 points, wins 9 points on 06-01 at Store One with an OMW% of 69%, to a total of 50 points.

Alice has the higher tiebreaker of her OMW% of 70% for the day.
