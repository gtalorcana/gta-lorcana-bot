## ⭐ #roles

⭐ **League Roles**

Roles reflect your competitive achievements in the GTA Lorcana League. They are awarded at the **end of each season** based on your final standing.

**The ladder:**

⬜ **Common** — League member

🟩 **Uncommon** — Attended 10+ events

🟧 **Rare** — Top 32 on the season leaderboard

🔷 **Super Rare** — Top 8 at an Invitational

👑 **Legendary** — Champion of an Invitational

✨ **Enchanted** — League organiser

**Getting your role:**

Roles are awarded at the end of each season based on your final standing. Enchanted and mod-appointed roles are assigned manually by Ryan.

Your Playhub username is used to match your results to your Discord account. I've done an initial pass to link accounts — if your role looks wrong or missing, message Ryan.

To help keep things accurate going forward, try to keep your **Discord display name consistent with your Playhub username**.

*Roles are never taken away — once earned, yours to keep.*

## 📋 #league-rules

📋 **League Rules & FAQ**

**What is the GTA Lorcana League?**

A seasonal league tracking player performances across the GTA. Top performers are invited to a **free** Invitational before the next set release.

**How long is a season?**

7 weeks, starting with a new set release and ending the day before Set Championships.

**How do I join?**

Play in your local store's constructed tournaments. Have someone submit results to #results-reporting after the event.

**What does my store need to do?**

Run tournaments with rounds and match results recorded. Prizing and structure are up to the store.

**What stores are participating?**

Any store can participate as long as someone is able to report results.

**Who organizes this?**

@gtalorcana and volunteers.

🎯 **Scoring**

Core Constructed tournaments only.

3 points per Match Win

1 point per Match Draw

0 points per Match Loss

Your final score is your **ten best performances** across the season.

Stores running four rounds are welcome to — a 4-0 earns 12 points. Stores running 5-round swiss with top cut: all match wins count.

🔢 **Tiebreakers**

When players are tied on points, tiebreakers are applied in this order:

**1st — Who reached the score first**

Alice hits 50 points on June 1. Bob hits 50 points on June 2. Alice wins the tiebreaker.

**2nd — Same points, same day — whose store reported first**

Both hit 50 points on June 1. Alice's store reported at 11:58 PM, Bob's at 11:59 PM. Alice wins the tiebreaker.

**3rd — Same points, same day, same store — who scored more points that day**

Alice earned 9 points on the day, Bob earned 6. Alice wins the tiebreaker.

**4th — Same points, same day, same store, same points earned — OMW%**

Alice had 70% OMW on the day, Bob had 69%. Alice wins the tiebreaker.

*All data is visible on the league spreadsheet for full transparency.*
